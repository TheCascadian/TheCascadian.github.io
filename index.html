About Us

Our Vision

As a collective born in the heart of the Pacific Northwest, we're a tight-knit family of artists pushing the boundaries of sound and creativity. Since 2020, we've been blending genres - from rap and hip-hop to pop and beyond - creating music that feels like home while breaking new ground. We believe in the raw power of authentic expression and the magic that happens when diverse artists come together to create something truly unique.

Our Mission

We strive to create a platform where artists can authentically express their emotions and connect with others through fresh, boundary-breaking sounds. Our collective represents a fusion of genres - from rap and hip-hop to hyperpop and alt-rock - creating music that feels like home while pushing creative limits. Together, we're exploring new sonic territories and inviting listeners to vibe with us.
